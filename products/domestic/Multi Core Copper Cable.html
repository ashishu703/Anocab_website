MULTICORE COPPER CABLE

TECHNICAL DATA

Reference: IS 694:2010
Voltage Grade: Up to and including 450/750Vs
Conductor: Electrolytic Grade Copper class – 2, 5, as per IS: 8130:2013
Insulation: PVC Type – A, HR PVC Type – C, as per IS 5831
Colour of Core: Red & black for 2 core cable, red, yellow, blue for 3 core cable, red, yellow, blue, black for 4 core cable
Colour of Sheath: Black and Other Colour as per Customer demand
Marking & Packing: Standard packing of 100/300/500 mtr. in coil. & Other length available on request, cables are printed with marking of ‘ANOCAB’

Applications

Power Distribution Systems: Ideal for supplying power in large industrial plants, commercial complexes, and infrastructure projects.
Control Panels & Equipment: Frequently used in control circuits and industrial machines where multiple signals need to be managed efficiently.
Telecommunications & Networking: Ensures stable data transmission and power supply in communication systems, networking installations, and data centers.
Automotive Wiring: Commonly used in automobiles and other vehicles for wiring multiple electrical circuits, ensuring dependable power distribution.
Building Management Systems: Employed in complex building systems for lighting, HVAC, and security controls.
Instrumentation and Control Systems: Widely used in measurement and control systems in industries such as oil & gas, power generation, and chemical processing.

Benefits

Enhanced Efficiency: Multi core cables carry multiple circuits, reducing the need for multiple single-core cables and optimizing space.
High Conductivity: Copper as a conductor ensures low resistance and high efficiency in power and signal transmission.
Durability & Longevity: Multi core copper cables are highly durable, resistant to corrosion, and provide long-lasting performance in various environments.
Space-Saving: By bundling multiple circuits in one cable, it reduces clutter and saves installation space, particularly in control panels and equipment.
Safety & Protection: High-quality insulation and sheath materials provide added protection against electrical faults, mechanical damage, and environmental hazards.
Cost-Effective: Reduces installation costs by simplifying wiring processes, decreasing labor, and minimizing the need for multiple cables.
