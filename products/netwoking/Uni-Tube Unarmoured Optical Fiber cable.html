UNI-TUBE UNARMOURED OPTICAL FIBER CABLE

TECHNICAL DATA

Reference: IEC.60794 series, ANSI/ICEA S-87-640, ITU-T Rec. G 652 D.
Tensile Strength: 500 N.
Bending Radius: 20 D.
Crush Resistance (N/100mm): 1000.
Impact Strength (N.m): 50 N 0.5 m.
Colours: Blue, Orange, Green, Brown, Grey, White, Red, Black, Yellow, Violet, Pink and Aqua.
Marking: The cables are printed with marking of ‘ANOCAB’.
Packing: 2000 mtr. coil is packed in protective Plastic bag.

APPLICATIONS

Power Distribution: Widely used for low-voltage power distribution in commercial and industrial settings, these cables ensure efficient energy transmission across multiple circuits.
Control Panels: Ideal for control wiring in panels, switchgear, and automation systems, providing reliable signal transmission and power supply to various components.
Building Wiring: Used in both residential and commercial buildings for wiring multiple electrical circuits, providing power to lights, outlets, and electrical equipment.
Renewable Energy: Often deployed in solar and wind energy systems, where multicore cables are essential for managing multiple electrical connections.
Automotive Applications: Suitable for automotive wiring systems where multicore cables are needed to manage various electrical circuits within the vehicle.
Instrumentation: Used in complex industrial instrumentation and control systems, facilitating the efficient transmission of signals and power between equipment.

BENEFITS

Lightweight: Aluminum is significantly lighter than copper, making these cables easier to handle and install, especially in large systems.
Cost-Effective: Multicore aluminum cables are an economical solution compared to copper cables, offering substantial savings without compromising performance.
Durable: The PVC insulation provides excellent protection against moisture, chemicals, and corrosion, ensuring long-term durability in various environmental conditions.
Space Saving: The multicore design combines multiple electrical circuits into a single cable, reducing installation space and simplifying complex wiring systems.
Heat Resistant: PVC insulation offers heat and flame-resistant properties, providing safety in environments where electrical cables may be exposed to high temperatures or fire risks.
Energy Efficient: While lighter than copper, aluminum maintains good electrical conductivity, ensuring efficient power transmission and reducing energy loss.
