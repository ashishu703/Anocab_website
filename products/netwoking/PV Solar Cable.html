PV SOLAR CABLE

TECHNICAL DATA

Reference: TUV : EN50618
Voltage Grade: Up to and including 600/1100 V AC, 1800 V DC
Conductor: Electrolytic Grade Annealed Tinned Copper Class – 5
Insulation: Cross linked Halogen Free and Flame Retardant Compound
Sheath: XLPO Compound Ozone and UV resistant
Colour: Red, black & other Colour on customer demand
Marking: The cables are printed with marking of ‘ANOCAB’
Packing: Standard packing of 100 mtr. in coil. & Other length available on request

APPLICATIONS

Solar Photovoltaic Systems: PV solar cables are specifically designed to connect solar panels to inverters and other system components, ensuring efficient energy transfer in solar photovoltaic (PV) systems.
Outdoor Solar Installations: These cables are ideal for outdoor solar installations, such as rooftop solar panels and ground-mounted solar arrays, where they endure exposure to various environmental conditions.
Energy Storage Systems: PV solar cables are used in energy storage systems to connect solar panels to batteries, enabling the storage of excess energy generated during the day for use during the night or on cloudy days.
Hybrid Systems: In hybrid renewable energy systems, PV cables connect solar panels to other power sources, such as wind turbines or generators, allowing for integrated energy management and optimized power supply.
Remote Off-Grid Applications: Used in off-grid solar installations, PV solar cables help provide energy to remote locations where traditional power sources are unavailable, supporting sustainable energy solutions in rural or isolated areas.

BENEFITS

High UV Resistance: Anocab’s PV solar cables are engineered to withstand prolonged exposure to sunlight, featuring UV-resistant insulation that ensures long-term durability and performance in outdoor applications.
Temperature Tolerance: These cables can operate effectively in a wide range of temperatures, maintaining their performance even in extreme heat or cold, which is essential for outdoor solar installations.
Low Voltage Drop: Designed for minimal electrical resistance, Anocab’s PV solar cables ensure low voltage drop, maximizing energy efficiency and performance in solar energy systems.
Flexible & Easy to Install: The flexibility of these cables makes them easy to handle and install, allowing for efficient routing and connections in various solar system configurations.
Chemical and Water Resistance: Anocab’s PV solar cables are resistant to moisture, chemicals, and environmental pollutants, enhancing their longevity and reliability in diverse operating conditions.
Compliance with Industry Standards: The cables comply with international safety and performance standards, ensuring reliable and safe operation in solar energy systems.
Cost-Effective Solution: By providing efficient energy transfer and durability, Anocab’s PV solar cables contribute to the overall cost-effectiveness of solar energy systems, reducing maintenance and replacement costs.
